title: "How billing works"
sidebarTitle: "Overview"
description: "You pay by the second for the time you use a phone, from a prepaid balance."
---

Axilio is **pay as you go**. You have a balance, and you spend from it as you use
phones.

- **Device time** is billed **per second**, from when you get a phone to when you
  return it.
- **Vision calls** (`find(query=...)`) cost a little extra, billed per call.
- Everything is itemized, so you always know
  [where your money went](/observability/cost).

Add funds and they're drawn down as you go. A plan can include a monthly balance
and set limits like how many runs you can do at once.
