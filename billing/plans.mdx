A plan sets your monthly included balance, how many runs you can do at once, and
your rates.

<div id="axilio-plans" />

These prices are pulled live from Axilio. See the
[pricing page](https://axilio.ai) for full details.

## Your current plan

```python
sub = client.billing.get_subscription()

print(sub.plan_name)
print(sub.included_balance_display)   # balance included each month
print(sub.max_concurrent_runs)        # how many runs at once
print(sub.current_period_end)
```

## Change your plan

Start a checkout for a new plan, then open the link to finish:

```python
checkout = client.billing.create_checkout(plan_id="plan_pro", billing_cycle="monthly")
print(checkout.checkout_url)
```

Find plan IDs and prices on the [pricing page](https://axilio.ai) or in your
[dashboard](https://axilio.ai/dashboard).
