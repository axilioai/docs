you one. Each kind of device has its own driver, with controls that fit it.

## How drivers fit in

- The **[Platform](/introduction)** gives you a device and manages it — the same
  for every kind of device.
- A **driver** controls that device. The controls match the device: a phone is a
  touchscreen, so the [Mobile driver](/driver/overview) is tap, type, and swipe.

Get started with the [Mobile driver](/driver/overview).
