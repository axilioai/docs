Axilio shows you everything: what your agents did, and what it cost. Every session
is recorded, every step is logged, and every cost is itemized. No black box.

## Where to see it

- **Dashboard**: live view, replays, and charts in your
  [dashboard](https://axilio.ai/dashboard).
- **SDK**: pull recordings, events, and costs from code.
- **Locally too**: even when you drive a phone from your own machine, the session
  is recorded and shows up the same way.
