Live view streams a phone's screen to your
[dashboard](https://axilio.ai/dashboard) in real time, so you can watch your agent
work as it happens.

Open any active session in the dashboard to see its live view. It's the fastest
way to see what your agent is doing right now, and to catch a problem while it's
still happening.

To review a session after it ends, use a [recording](/observability/recordings)
instead.
