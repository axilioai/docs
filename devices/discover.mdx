## What's free

`available` shows how many phones are free to borrow right now:

```python
free = client.phones.available()
print(free.android_count, "Android phones free")
```

## Your phones

`mine` lists the phones tied to your account, like
[dedicated ones you rented](/billing/plans):

```python
mine = client.phones.mine()

for phone in mine.phones:
    print(phone.phone_id, phone.nickname, phone.status)
```

Pass a phone's `phone_id` to [`session`](/devices/allocate) to use that exact
phone.
