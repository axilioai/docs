By default you borrow any free phone. A **dedicated device** is a specific phone
you rent, so it's yours around the clock, with no waiting for one to be free.

- It's the **same phone every time**, so apps stay logged in and set up the way
  you left them.
- You pay a **flat rental** (daily, weekly, or monthly) instead of per second.
  Sessions on a dedicated phone cost **$0**.

## Rent one

Dedicated phones are billed as a flat rental:

<div id="axilio-rental-plans" />

Start a checkout for a rental plan, then open the link to finish:

```python
checkout = client.billing.create_rental_checkout(plan_id="rental_monthly")
print(checkout.checkout_url)
```

Find rental plans in your [dashboard](https://axilio.ai/dashboard).

## Use your dedicated phone

Your rented phones show up in `devices.mine()`. Pass a phone's `phone_id` to
`session()` to use that exact phone:

```python
mine = client.phones.mine()

for phone in mine.phones:
    if phone.rental_expires_at:   # your dedicated phones
        print(phone.nickname, phone.phone_id, phone.rental_expires_at)

with client.session("ANDROID", phone_id="<your phone_id>") as driver:
    ...
```

## Manage your rentals

```python
subs = client.billing.list_rental_subscriptions()

client.billing.renew_rental_subscriptions(subscription_ids=["sub_123"])
client.billing.cancel_rental_subscriptions(subscription_ids=["sub_123"])
```
