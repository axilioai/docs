A **device** is a real Android phone in Axilio's cloud. A **session** is a phone
you've borrowed to use.

You don't keep phones. You borrow one, do your thing, and give it back. You only
pay for the time you hold it.

```python
from axilio.platform import Client

client = Client()

with client.session("ANDROID") as driver:
    ...   # the phone is yours inside this block
# it's returned automatically here
```

## How it fits with the driver

The Platform gives you the phone. The [driver](/driver/overview) controls it.
`session()` does both: it borrows a phone and hands you a driver to drive it.
