An **organization** is your account on Axilio. Your members, API keys, devices,
runs, and billing all belong to it.

Each [API key](/platform/api-keys) is tied to one organization, so everything you
do with that key counts against that org's balance and shows up in its history. If
you're in more than one organization, use the key for the one you mean to work in.
