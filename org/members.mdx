Invite teammates to share an organization. Manage members in your
[dashboard](https://axilio.ai/dashboard), or from the SDK.

<Note>
  The SDK calls below take your organization ID, which you can find in your
  dashboard settings.
</Note>

## Invite a teammate

```python
client.org.create_invitation(
    org_id="org_123",
    email="teammate@example.com",
    role="member",
)
```

## List members

```python
members = client.org.list_members(org_id="org_123")
for member in members.members:
    print(member)
```

## Change a role or remove someone

```python
client.org.update_member_role(org_id="org_123", user_id="user_456", role="admin")
client.org.remove_member(org_id="org_123", user_id="user_456")
```
