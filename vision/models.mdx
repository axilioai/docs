Argus runs on a range of vision models. List them in code:

```python
models = client.argus.list_models()
for m in models.data:
    print(m.name, m.context_window)
```

Pass a model's `id` as `model=` to [Locate](/vision/locate) to pick one.

## Pricing

Vision calls are billed per token.
