**Argus** is Axilio's vision model. It looks at an image and finds things by
description, reads the text, and spots the icons.

You've already used it: [`find(query=...)`](/driver/find) on the driver is Argus
under the hood. You can also call it directly on any image with `client.argus`.

## When to call it directly

The [driver](/driver/find) already runs Argus for you on a live screen. Call
`client.argus` yourself when you have your own image to analyze.
