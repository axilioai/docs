Swipe between two points on the screen. This is also how you scroll:

```python
driver.swipe({"x": 540, "y": 1600}, {"x": 540, "y": 400})   # scroll down
```

<Note>
  Swiping is how you scroll: swipe **up** (end higher than start) to move *down* a
  list, and the other way to move up. Pass `duration_ms` for a slower, shorter
  swipe.
</Note>

## Swipe one thing onto another

Swipe from something you [found](/driver/find) to another, for example to drag a
card onto a target:

```python
driver.find_text("Card").swipe_to(driver.find_text("Archive"))
```
