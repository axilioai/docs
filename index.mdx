Axilio gives your agents a real place to work: genuine Android phones on live carrier networks, allocated on demand, driven by a vision-native SDK, and observable at every step. No emulators, no scripted browsers, no mocked networks.

```python
from axilio.platform import Client
from axilio.drivers import MobileDriver

client = Client()

with client.mobile.session(platform="android") as device:
    driver = MobileDriver(device)
    driver.find_text("Sign in").tap()
    driver.find(query="the blue Continue button").tap()
```
