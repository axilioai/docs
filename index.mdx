Axilio gives your agents **real Android phones in the cloud** — and a simple Python
library to control them. Pick up a phone, read its screen, and tap, type, and
swipe, just like a person would.

Most apps can't be plugged into directly — they have no public API. So an agent
uses them the same way you do: on a real phone. That's what Axilio is for.
