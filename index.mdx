Axilio gives your agents **real Android phones in the cloud** — genuine hardware on
live carrier networks — and a vision-native Python SDK to drive them. Allocate a
device, see the screen, and tap, type, and swipe your way through any app.

Most mobile apps have **no public API**. The only way an agent can use them is to
drive a real phone, the same way a person does. That's what Axilio is for.
